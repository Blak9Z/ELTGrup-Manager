# User Guide

This guide explains the primary workflows in ELTGRUP Manager.

## 1. Project Management Flow

### Creating a Project
1.  Navigate to **Proiecte**.
2.  Click **Adauga proiect**.
3.  Fill in the project details: Title, Client, Manager, Estimated Budget, and Dates.
4.  Once created, click the project title to view its dashboard.

### Adding Phases and Work Orders
Within a project page, you can create **Phases** (milestones) and then add **Work Orders** (tasks) to those phases. Each work order can be assigned to a specific person and a team.

## 2. Field Operations (Teren)

The **Teren** module is optimized for mobile use by workers on-site.

### Daily Check-in
1.  Open the app on a mobile device and go to **Teren**.
2.  Click **Check-in santier**. The app will optionally record your GPS coordinates.
3.  At the end of the day, click **Check-out santier**.

### Live Time Tracking
1.  Find your assigned task in the **Task-uri Azi** list.
2.  Enter an optional note and click **Începe Lucrul**. This starts a live timer.
3.  You can pause or stop the timer at any time from the **Tura Activa** panel.

### Reporting Progress
1.  In the **Raport Teren** section, select the project and task.
2.  Fill in the daily progress, any blockers, and weather conditions.
3.  Click **Trimite Raport**.

### Uploading Proof of Work
1.  Scroll to the **Foto & Semnătură** section.
2.  Select the relevant task.
3.  Use the **Foto Progres** input to take a photo directly with your camera.
4.  Upload signatures or delivery notes (PV) in the same way.

## 3. Time Tracking & Approvals

### Reviewing Timesheets
Managers and Accountants can go to **Pontaj** to see all recorded time.
-   Filter by project, user, or date.
-   Identify entries in **SUBMITTED** status.

### Bulk Approvals
1.  Check the boxes next to the entries you want to approve.
2.  Select **Aproba selectie** from the bulk actions menu.
3.  Click **Executa bulk**.

## 4. Materials & Logistics

-   **Materiale**: Manage the master catalog and track stock levels across warehouses.
-   **Gestiune scule**: Track assigned tools and inventory items.
-   **Stock Movements**: Every material movement (IN, OUT, TRANSFER) is recorded to maintain an accurate audit trail and project cost calculation.
